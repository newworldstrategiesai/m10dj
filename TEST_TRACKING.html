  1. Open your website: http://localhost:3000

  4. Click any of these tracked elements:

✅ Service dropdown links → trackServiceInterest(service_name)

🎯 Homepage Hero Section Enhanced
✅ "Get Your Free Quote" → trackLead('quote_request_start', {source: 'hero_section'})
✅ "View Our Services" → trackServiceInterest('all_services', 'hero_section')
✅ "Sign In" button → trackLead('admin_signin_attempt')
📋 Contact Form Enhanced
✅ Form submissions → trackLead('contact_form') + Facebook Lead event
